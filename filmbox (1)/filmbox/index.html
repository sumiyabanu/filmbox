Frequently asked questions


What is FilmBox+? + FilmBox+ is an entertainment service where you can watch hundreds of movies and series online with a single subscription. You may also watch live channels with your subscription.
Why should I subscribe? + FilmBox+ offers a large catalogue of content spanning all genres including Hollywood movies, favorite evergreens, worldcinema gems, popular TV series, all in one place and completely ad-free! You can browse through our catalogue without having to subscribe. However, in order to watch all the content and create a favorites list you need a subscription.
How can I subscribe?+ Registration is very easy. Register on our website: Go to www.filmbox.com, click on the 'Login' button located at the header. Click on the 'Sign Up' button, follow the steps outlined in the next page for registration. Register on our application: Install the FilmBox+ application, click on the Menu icon on the top left of the screen. Click on 'Log In', scroll down and click 'Sign Up'. Fill in the form and click on the 'Sign Up' button to finalize your registration. You can also subscribe using your Google Play, iTunes/Apple or Facebook accounts.
What is '7-days free trial'?+ In order to experience FilmBox+, we give you a 7-days free trial. During the free trial, you can use all the features and watch unlimited content. You can cancel your free trial at any time.
How can I cancel my subscription?+ Please follow the steps below to cancel your FilmBox+ subscription. If you subscribed with your Google Play or Apple App Store (iTunes) account, you can cancel your FilmBox+ subscription using this account. If you subscribed with a credit card, you can cancel your subscription via the 'My Account' menu. When you visit the menu, you will see the option to cancel. If you are having trouble canceling your subscription, please send an email with the subject 'Cancellation' to support@filmbox.com. Our representatives will assist you with your request. If you have a FilmBox+ subscription through another service provider, please contact the customer service of that service provider to make the cancellation.
